Professional Profile

Mark Collins

Mark Collins is an independent luxury hospitality advisor with over two decades of executive leadership across independent hotels, branded collections, and investment-grade hospitality assets. His practice is built on a single discipline: structured performance improvement and owner-side representation, conducted with full accountability to ownership.

His background includes senior leadership at The Standard, High Line in New York City and Island Outpost Hotels, alongside executive assignments across Europe, the United States, and the Caribbean — providing sustained exposure to complex ownership structures and assets operating at the upper tier of the international luxury market.

He approaches hospitality assets from the perspective of the investor and owner — with rigorous attention to P&L architecture, cost control, CAPEX governance, and management accountability. His operating style is discreet, structured, and defined by clear deliverables.

Engagements are accepted on a limited and selective basis. An initial confidential conversation is the standard first step for all prospective assignments.
